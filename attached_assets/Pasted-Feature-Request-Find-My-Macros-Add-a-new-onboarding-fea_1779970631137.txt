Feature Request: “Find My Macros”

Add a new onboarding feature in Peppies called “Find My Macros”.

Purpose

Help users quickly calculate their daily calorie and macro targets based on their body stats and fitness goal.

This should feel simple, fast, and beginner-friendly.

⸻

User Flow

Create a step-by-step onboarding screen called Find My Macros.

Ask the user for:

* Age
* Sex
* Height
* Current Weight
* Goal Weight (optional)
* Activity Level
* Goal

Activity Level options:

* Sedentary
* Lightly Active
* Moderately Active
* Very Active
* Athlete / Extremely Active

Goal options:

* Lose Fat
* Maintain Weight
* Build Muscle
* Body Recomposition (lose fat while maintaining/building muscle)

⸻

Calculator Logic

1. Calculate BMR using Mifflin–St Jeor equation

Male
BMR = (10 × weight in kg) + (6.25 × height in cm) − (5 × age) + 5

Female
BMR = (10 × weight in kg) + (6.25 × height in cm) − (5 × age) − 161

⸻

2. Calculate TDEE

Multiply BMR by activity multiplier:

* Sedentary = 1.2
* Lightly Active = 1.375
* Moderately Active = 1.55
* Very Active = 1.725
* Athlete = 1.9

This gives the user’s maintenance calories.

⸻

3. Apply Goal Adjustment

Based on goal selected:

Lose Fat
→ TDEE - 300 to 500 calories

Maintain
→ TDEE

Build Muscle
→ TDEE + 150 to 300 calories

Body Recomposition
→ maintenance or slight deficit depending on user weight

⸻

Macro Calculation

Automatically generate:

Protein

0.9 grams per pound of bodyweight

Fat

0.35 grams per pound of bodyweight

Carbs

Use remaining calories after protein and fat are calculated.

Formula:

Carbs = remaining calories ÷ 4

⸻

Results Screen

After calculation, show a clean summary card:

Your Peppies Daily Targets 🎯

Calories: XXXX

Protein: XXXg

Carbs: XXXg

Fat: XXg

Text below:

“Based on your stats and goal, these are your recommended daily targets. You can edit these anytime in Settings.”

⸻

Save to User Profile

Save calculated values as the user’s default daily targets:

* Daily Calories
* Protein Goal
* Carb Goal
* Fat Goal

Use these throughout the app dashboard and food tracker.

⸻

Editing

Allow users to manually edit their macro targets later inside Settings if they want custom numbers.

⸻

Design Style

Keep the design aligned with Peppies:

* clean
* modern
* minimal
* easy to understand
* mobile-first

Make it feel quick and encouraging—not overly technical.

The goal is for users to feel like:

“Peppies figured out my targets for me in under 30 seconds.”
